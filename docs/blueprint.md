# **App Name**: Sante-Price Index

## Core Features:

- Mandi Price Viewer: Displays up-to-date city Mandi prices for key produce items like onion and tomato, fetched from a central database.
- Intelligent Profit Calculator: Allows vendors to input variable transport and waste costs to calculate a Recommended Retail Price (RRP), ensuring competitive and profitable sales.
- Digital Price Board Display: Provides a high-contrast (e.g., yellow on black), full-screen display of current RRPs for easy readability by customers from a distance.
- AI-powered Price Trend Tool: Generates simulated insights and predictions on whether produce prices are likely to rise or fall, empowering vendors to anticipate market shifts.
- Firebase Data Integration: Integrates with a Firebase backend to efficiently fetch daily Mandi prices, ensuring data freshness and reliability.

## Style Guidelines:

- Dark color scheme with a deep, almost black, background (#1F1A14) to enhance readability and align with the 'Digital Slate' aesthetic. This background has a subtle warmth, drawn from the primary hue, ensuring sophistication over starkness.
- Primary color: A vibrant orange-gold (#FE9A09) used for key information, interactive elements, and headings, chosen to evoke a sense of value, warmth, and modern data-driven insight, ensuring high visibility against the dark background.
- Accent color: A soft, bright peachy-red (#FDAMA0) for calls to action, highlights, and secondary interactive elements. Its lightness and hue provide a distinct contrast to the primary color, guiding user focus effectively.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its clean, neutral, and highly legible characteristics, ensuring all pricing information and instructional text is easy to read from a distance, supporting the app's 'rugged and simple' aesthetic.
- Use clear, bold, and illustrative icons for produce items (e.g., onion, tomato) and functional actions (e.g., calculate, trends). Icons should be simple to interpret at a glance, reflecting the straightforward nature of the app's purpose.
- Prioritize clear hierarchy and minimal visual clutter, especially for the 'Digital Price Board' which should be full-screen with high-contrast text. Data entry forms should be intuitive and direct, minimizing steps for the vendor.
- Implement subtle, functional animations such as smooth transitions between different views or a focused loading indicator during price calculations and data fetching, enhancing the sense of responsiveness without distracting the user.
